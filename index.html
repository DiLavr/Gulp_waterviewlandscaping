Our Portfolio

As you explore our project portfolio and testimonials you will notice that we do not prioritize our projects as big or small, they are all of equal importance to us. The larger projects are exciting, naturally, however sometimes the smallest project results in a truly inspirational space.

Waterview Landscaping considers all of the individual elements within the structure of the landscape, such as the pool or a fire pit, as a part of the complete design layout.

The pool is one of the most elegant elements within the landscape. Acting as the jewel shining from the distance it is important for the color and texture of the natural landscape to soften and support the pool area drawing you into and through the space and beyond. These elements should not feel disjointed or arbitrary.
We strive to weave these signature elements into the landscape as organically as possible.

There is no such thing as a big or small project; Waterview Landscaping is happy to restore the lawn at your property as well as build the outdoor kitchen.
